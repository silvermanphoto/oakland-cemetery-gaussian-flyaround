Two choices that are yours to make

  • Corridor width — keep 30 m (central tracks), widen to ~50 m to swallow the full 45.8 m rail bundle (all parallel tracks), or narrow to just the main pair. One parameter.
  • Connection — leave the ~14 m gap so the rail strip floats just off the cemetery, or bridge it so the kept rail is one continuous piece with the cemetery.
